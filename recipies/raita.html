Kumaoni Raita is a delicious cucumber raita

Pahadi kheere ka raita

Ingredients

  • 1 large cucumber, peeled and grated
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon black salt (kala namak)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1 tablespoon fresh coriander leaves, finely chopped
  • Salt to taste

    • Instructions

    1. In a bowl, take the grated cucumber and sprinkle some salt over it. Mix well and let it sit for about 5 minutes. This step helps to remove excess water from the cucumber.
    2. After 5 minutes, squeeze out the water from the grated cucumber using your hands or a clean cloth. Discard the water.
    3. In another bowl, whisk the plain yogurt until smooth and creamy. Add the grated cucumber to the yogurt and mix well.
    4. Add roasted cumin powder, black salt, black pepper powder, and fresh coriander leaves to the cucumber-yogurt mixture. Stir everything together until well combined.
    5. Taste the raita and adjust the seasoning according to your preference. You can add more salt or spices if desired.
    6. Once the raita is well mixed and seasoned, refrigerate it for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
    7. Before serving, give the raita a good stir. Garnish with a sprinkle of roasted cumin powder and fresh coriander leaves.
    8. Serve Pahadi Kheere Ka Raita chilled as a side dish with your favorite Indian meals, such as biryani, pulao, or parathas
